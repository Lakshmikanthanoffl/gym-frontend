Our Services

Last updated: September 22, 2025

We provide a complete suite of services to make gym management effortless for gym owners. Our platform is designed to help owners digitalize, streamline, and manage all aspects of their fitness center operations.

  • Centralized management of gym members & subscriptions
  • Flexible subscription plan setup (monthly, quarterly, yearly)
  • Trainer & staff scheduling and management
  • Attendance & activity tracking
  • Automated reminders & invoicing for gym operations
  • Analytics dashboard for revenue, expenses & growth insights
  • Secure subscription payments by gym owners to Zyct via Razorpay
